Our Services

Experience the best in hair care and styling with our professional services

Hair cuts

Get the perfect cut tailored to your style and personality. Our experienced stylists take the time to understand your preferences and create a look that complements your face shape and lifestyle. Whether you want a classic trim or a trendy new style, we ensure you leave the salon feeling confident and refreshed.

Price: $40-60

Highlights, root touch ups & Colour

Add dimension and brightness to your hair with our expert highlights. Our skilled colorists use the latest techniques to ensure your highlights look natural and blend seamlessly with your base color. Maintain your color flawlessly with our precise root touch-up services, keeping your hair vibrant and healthy.

Price: $70-250

Treatments

Revitalize your hair with our nourishing treatments for a healthy, shiny look. Our treatments are designed to address specific hair concerns, from dryness and damage to frizz and split ends. Enjoy a relaxing experience as our specialists apply customized treatments that leave your hair feeling soft, strong, and rejuvenated.

Price: $60-120

Hair styling

Enhance your look with our professional hair styling services for any occasion. Whether you're preparing for a special event or simply want to look your best, our stylists are adept at creating a wide range of styles, from elegant updos to sleek blowouts. We use top-quality products to ensure your style holds and looks great all day.

Price: $50-100

Keratin Treatment

Smooth and straighten your hair with our premium keratin treatment. This advanced treatment infuses your hair with keratin, reducing frizz and adding a beautiful shine. Ideal for those seeking a long-lasting, manageable hairstyle, our keratin treatment will leave your hair looking smooth, healthy, and effortlessly stylish.

Price: $150-300

Scalp Treatment

Rejuvenate your scalp with our specialized treatments to promote healthy hair growth. Our scalp treatments target issues such as dryness, dandruff, and irritation, providing relief and promoting a healthier environment for hair growth. Enjoy a soothing massage and effective treatment that leaves your scalp feeling refreshed and your hair looking its best.

Price: $80-150
